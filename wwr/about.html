Hossana Water Rafting Co.

At Hossana Water Rafting Co., our goal is to deliver extraordinary nature excursions and strive to turn every river trip into a remarkable journey by blending first class safety with pure excitement. We foster a spirit of adventure, environmental respect, embracing the wild and leaving you with stories to tell for a lifetime.

History

Established in 2015, the business began as a modest riverside outfitter known as Hossana Rafting. Over the decade, it evolved into a premier adventure brand, providing thrilling expeditions and outdoor experiences to global travelers. That same pioneering spirit and drive for innovation continue to guide the company today.
